# VogueVessel - Lifestyle Blog

A comprehensive lifestyle blog website featuring 6 categories: Fashion & Accessories, Health & Beauty, Home & Garden, Travel & Accommodation, Finance & Insurance, and Food & Beverage.

## Features

### Design
- **Flat Design Style**: Clean, modern interface with vibrant colors and simple shapes
- **No 3D Effects**: Pure flat design without gradients, shadows, or embossing
- **VogueVessel Logo**: Prominent branding throughout the site
- **Bold Color Palette**: Eye-catching colors for different categories

### Functionality
- **Category Filtering**: Filter blog posts by 6 different categories
- **Search Function**: Search articles by keywords
- **Mobile Responsive**: Fully responsive design for all devices
- **Smooth Animations**: Elegant transitions and hover effects

### Content
- **5 Blog Articles** with images (dated January 2025 - August 2025):
  1. Top 10 Summer Fashion Trends (Fashion - August 2025)
  2. Natural Skincare Routine (Beauty - June 2025)
  3. Modern Home Decor Ideas (Home - May 2025)
  4. Hidden Travel Destinations (Travel - March 2025)
  5. Healthy Meal Prep Ideas (Food - January 2025)

- **Product Recommendations**: Each article includes product showcases with ratings and prices
- **High-Quality Images**: Realistic images from Unsplash that match article content
- **Detailed Content**: Medium-length articles with less than 5 images each

### Pages
- **Home Page** (`index.html`): Main landing page with all blog posts
- **Search Results** (`search.html`): Dedicated search results page with smart filtering
- **Blog Articles** (`blog/article1-5.html`): Individual article pages
- **About Page** (`about.html`): Information about VogueVessel
- **Contact Page** (`contact.html`): Contact form and information
- **Privacy Policy** (`privacy-policy.html`): Comprehensive privacy policy and data protection information

### Additional Features
- Social media widget with links
- Smooth scroll animations
- Category cards with icons
- Footer with quick links
- Mobile menu toggle

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser
2. Browse articles by category using the filter buttons
3. Use the search bar to find specific content - results will open in a dedicated search page
4. Click on article cards to read full articles
5. Navigate to About and Contact pages via the header menu

### Search Feature
- Enter keywords in the search bar on the home page
- Press Enter or click the search button
- View results on the dedicated search results page
- Search covers article titles, descriptions, categories, and keywords
- Perform new searches directly from the search results page
